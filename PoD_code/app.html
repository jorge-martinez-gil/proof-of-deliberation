How to get PoD ✓

  • Click a row, then open the Features dropdown at least once.
  • Wait until the PoD timer badge turns green (usually 0.8s to 2.5s).
  • If Non-OK, enter a short reason (3 to 10 words) before selecting status.
  • Avoid rhythmic clicks across rows. Natural variation is good.

Why PoD can fail

  • Status chosen too quickly after first interaction.
  • No feature inspection signal (dropdown never opened).
  • Stream in Caution mode: repeated fast commits detected.
  • Very low variability in timing for many consecutive rows.
